TVS Apache enters Asia Book of Records with a 6 hour non-stop stunt marathon

TVS Apache Series has created history by entering the Asia and India Book of Records for the longest non-stop stunt show for 6 hours in India, after breaking their own previous record of 5 hours of non - stop stunting. As part of the Apache Pro Performance X, 5 stunt teams performed 6 hours straight with their Apache RTR 200 4V race machines despite extreme weather conditions in Pune. Congratulations to the entire team for this phenomenal achievement

TVS Motor Company launches TVS Apache RTR 200 4V with first-in-segment Ride Modes

TVS Motor Company introduced its new TVS Apache RTR 200 4V (dual-channel ABS) motorcycle with innovative segment-first features, engineered with three ride modes, namely Sport, Urban and Rain. The motorcycle is equipped with first-in-segment adjustable suspension and levers. The motorcycle stands tall to its promise of delivering cutting edge technology, using years of racing experience and pedigree, to celebrate the brand's 4 million global sales milestone.

TVS Apache enters the Asia Book of Records for creating the Longest Chequered Flag

To commemorate the 4 million milestone celebrations, TVS Apache in collaboration with its customers has created the "Longest Chequered Flag" spanning a length of 957ft x 5ft. Made up of customer sourced images from across the globe, the flag is an homage to the esteemed customers for their love and faith in the brand. With over 2,000 images being used to create this masterpiece, the flag has made its entry in both, Asia Book of Records and India Book of Records on 7th October 2020. Click here to know more

TVS Motor Company introduces the new generation of the 2020 TVS Apache RR 310 BS-VI motorcycle in 2 colours. The newly launched race machine will further move the needle by creating a technological benchmark with many segment first features and tech such as Throttle-by-wire, SmartXonnect, 4 riding modes, GTT+ thereby strengthening its status of being the best-in-class vehicle.

TVS Motor Company launches the 2020 TVS Apache RTR 160 BS-VI motorcycle

TVS Motor Company introduces the 2020 TVS Apache RTR 160 BS-VI. The bike is equipped with updated race graphics, ensuring a sportier appeal and is powered by the Race Tuned-Fuel injection 'RT-Fi' technology. The 2020 TVS Apache RTR 160 BS-VI comes with an in-built, first in segment GTT - Glide Through Technology for low-speed urban riding and enables an extremely smooth and controlled ride.

TVS Motor Company introduces the 2020 range of TVS Apache RTR 4V Series

TVS Motor Company introduces the all-new 2020 range of TVS Apache RTR 200 4V and TVS Apache RTR 160 4V. The 2020 Apache RTR 4V series comes with RT-Fi engine and will offer an array of enhanced features like New LED Headlamp, Feather touch start, New Race Graphics. This is in line with the philosophy of the TVS Apache series that has revolutionised performance biking by introducing cutting edge technology from their factory racing versions.

TVS Apache stunt riders create history and enter Asia Book of Records!

Set at an altitude of over 14,800 ft above sea level and temperature dropping down to 3 degree Celsius, Three TVS Racing factory stunt riders achieved a feat, which no team has dared to dream. The record-breaking show was held in a controlled environment as a part of the Apache Owners Group (AOG) ride from Chandigarh to Spiti Valley.
